🏛️ About Us

Trusted by Thousands of Patients

FaceHealth Medical Center is a NABH-accredited multi-specialty hospital established in 2018, pioneering the use of AI-powered face recognition for seamless patient management. Our mission is to make healthcare faster, more accessible, and digitally secure.
